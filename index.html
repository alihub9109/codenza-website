Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you understand how we work at Codenza.

How long does it take to build a website?
Most of our one-page websites are completed in just 3–5 days, depending on how fast we receive your content and feedback.
What do your website packages include?
Each website includes custom design, responsive layout for all screen sizes, performance optimization, basic SEO, and full delivery on a live link. You can also add extra services like content writing or future updates.
Is hosting included?
Yes. We provide free, fast hosting using Netlify, including SSL for secure HTTPS. If you prefer a custom domain like yourname.com, we can help you set it up at an additional cost.
Can you help with domain setup?
Absolutely. We guide you through buying and connecting a custom domain. We can also fully handle it for you if you'd rather not deal with the technical side.
What if I want to change something after the website is done?
We offer optional maintenance and update plans. Whether it's updating text, adding a new section, or making small design changes—we’ve got you covered.
